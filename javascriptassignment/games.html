Rules
Players swipe to move all tiles in a chosen direction (up, down, left, or right), with identical tiles merging into one higher-valued tile.
After each move, a new tile (either a 2 or a 4) appears in a random empty space.
The game ends when no valid moves—either tile movements or combinations—are possible
